Outcome

  • Stakeholders adopted the new flow structure across multiple promotional campaigns.
  • Marketing teams were able to reuse plan modules instead of requesting new templates for every offer.
  • Product and engineering had clearer, more stable flows to support experimentation and A/B testing.

What I Learned

Enterprise-scale work benefits from thinking in systems, not pages. Designing flexible frameworks for plans and promos reduced friction for users and lowered the cost of change for the team.
