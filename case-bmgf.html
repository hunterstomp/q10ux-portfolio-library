My Role

  • UX strategy and end-to-end flow design

Constraints

  • Legacy system constraints and phased releases
  • Cross-team dependencies
  • Timeboxed delivery

Process

Research

I reviewed existing journeys, support drivers, and analytics signals to pinpoint the highest-friction steps.

Information Architecture

I reorganized the information so the user’s top questions are answered first, then details and edge cases follow in a scannable structure.

Wireframes & Prototyping

I translated requirements into wireframes and clickable prototypes to test sequencing, clarity, and confirmation states before build.

Iterations

I iterated language, hierarchy, and state design with stakeholders, tightening the flow until it felt obvious under real-world pressure.
