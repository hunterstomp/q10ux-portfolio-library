Outcome

  • Playback behavior is easier to predict, especially for first-time users.
  • Support and internal teams have a clearer mental model for how modes and playlists function.
  • The player UI scales more gracefully as new content types are added.

What I Learned

Creative tools still need rigorous structure. Reducing control ambiguity gave users more confidence to experiment with their displays.
